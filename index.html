The law firm of Shumway Van was founded by attorneys Michael C. Van and Douglas J. Shumway in 2008 in response to business owners and friends expressing concerns about not being able to afford the large firms they worked with for years because those law firms were still charging as though the economy was strong even though the Great Recession had decimated the profits of many businesses and dramatically impacted many employees of those businesses.

Our Practice Areas

Shumway Van is a full-service law firm with offices in Nevada, Texas, and Utah.
